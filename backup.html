How to backup

Inspr now supports iCloud Drive, therefore you can access all your files from the iCloud Drive app or iCloud.com. iCloud is an Apple's technic to synchronize app's files cross devices. If you have a Mac device, it will be easier to access all your files. If not, no need to worry, you still can download your files from iCloud.com

For Mac devices

If you are using Mac device and have signed in with your Apple Id, you can access all your iCloud files on Finder:
If you don't see iCloud Drive, make sure you have turned on the iCloud Drive feature.
All your files are in the "Inspr" folder. When you go into the "Inspr" app folder, you will find several sub folders. They also represent the projects you created in Inspr. The folder name is the name of a project.
If you want to back files, just select the files or folders you want to backup, then compress and save the compression to somewhere else like Dropbox or hardrive of your Mac.
To restore files, just simply copy your backup (must be the files with inspr file extension) under any sub folder of Inspr on iCloud Drive. And since each sub folder also represents a project in the app, you must place the backup under one of sub folders. Nested sub folder is not allowed.

For PC or Windows

If you are using Windows or other PC platforms, there are two approaches to access your files. One is to login Apple's website iCloud.com. Another way is to download iCloud Drive client from Apple's website.
If you choose to use iCloud.com, you can login with your Apple ID and password. Once you login, tap into the iCloud Drive service. If you don't see it, please check this website for the troubleshooting.
When you go into iCloud Drive, and you will find Inspr folder. If not, please check this website for trouble shooting.
In iCloud.com, you can download to backup locally. Or upload your backup to restore it (place it under any project folder).
